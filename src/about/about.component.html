About Our 3D Configurator

Democratizing 3D content creation and customization for everyone.

This 3D Material Editor and Configurator was born from a passion for 3D art and web technology. Our mission is to provide an accessible, powerful, and intuitive tool that empowers creators to bring their digital visions to life directly in their browser. Whether you're a seasoned 3D artist, a game developer, a product designer, or just a hobbyist, this tool is designed for you.

We believe that creativity shouldn't be locked behind expensive software or steep learning curves. By leveraging the power of modern web technologies, we've created a seamless experience that runs on any device without installation.

Core Technologies

Our application is built on a robust, modern tech stack to ensure performance and reliability:

  • Angular: A leading framework for building high-performance, scalable single-page applications.
  • Three.js: The powerful 3D graphics library that renders our scenes and models directly in the browser.
  • Gemini API: The cutting-edge AI that powers our generative material feature, turning simple text prompts into complex PBR textures.
  • Tailwind CSS: A utility-first CSS framework that allows for rapid, responsive UI development.

We are constantly working to improve the tool and add new features. Thank you for being a part of our journey!
